## How it works

You get a personal link `monty.fast/r/YOUR_CODE` and a coupon. Every customer who arrives through them and pays for Monty earns you a commission - **30% of every payment, for life**, as long as they keep paying. Larger partners get a higher rate.

Always include your link or code: without them, payments don't credit to you.

Current terms and signup are on [monty.fast](https://monty.fast). Questions: team@monty.fast.

## Using the assets

Files in `promokit-assets/` are yours to use for promoting Monty: posts, stories, reels, thumbnails. Not allowed: redrawing the mark, changing logo proportions, inventing your own mascots, buying branded ads on "Monty".
